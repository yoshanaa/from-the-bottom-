the story

why i built this.

01

my first ever breakup. with someone i loved a lot. and it wasn't just a breakup - it was three months of genuinely not knowing how to be okay without that person.

02

i was leaning on my friends and family a lot. too much, honestly. and i started to wonder - is there something i can do just for me? something quiet. something that's just mine.

03

so i built this. a simple place to check in with myself every day. no advice, no one else's opinion - just me, a number, and whatever was on my mind.

04

and then something happened. i started seeing my own data. some days the graph went up. some days it didn't. and on the days it didn't, i'd ask myself - do i actually want to make this breakup my entire personality?

that question changed things.

05

i reflected. then reflected again. and slowly - not all at once, not in a straight line - the graph started going up more than it went down.

this site is still growing. there's more i want to add, more features, more ways to track the rebuild. but i started with the basics - because sometimes that's all you need to start seeing yourself clearly again.
